About WkndMVP

A Community for Makers

WkndMVP is a community for makers that was founded by the r/startup Reddit community. WkndMVP is about bringing our complementary skills together over the weekends to do what we love - create new, smart things.

Date & Duration

The eHackathon starts:

Thurs Aug 2 @ 12:00 PST & runs until Sun, Aug 23 @ 23:59 PST

Submission must be entered online no later than Sun, Aug 23 @ 23:59 PST.

Submission

Team leaders log in at wkndmvp.com and submit your team project before the deadline Sun, Aug 23 @ 23:59 PST.

Voting

  1. After the eHackathon deadline Community reviews submissions & choose best of or 1/2/3 for each category.
  2. 1 week for community to vote
  3. Voters sign in with reddit to cast vote, 1 vote per username per category?
  4. Voters cannot vote on their own project
  5. Account must be 1 week old from the start of hackathon
  6. Account must belong to r startups subreddit
